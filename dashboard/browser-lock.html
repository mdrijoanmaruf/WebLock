Browser Lock

Browser Lock protects your entire browser with a password. When enabled, you'll be prompted to enter your password every time you launch your browser.

If enabled, you won't be asked for password on this device.

Tips:

  • You can quickly lock your browser from the extension popup.
  • When tab restoration is enabled, all your tabs will be restored when you unlock the browser.
  • The "Remember This Device" option will skip the password prompt on this specific device.
